Introduction
DoC is a one-day free design event which is hold once every year. It focused on resolving problems suffered by designers in their daily works. Each year we invited three famous guest speakers to provide presentations on different problems. Participators can join the discussion after the presentation and communicate their ideas with others. We aimed to make a chance for designers gathering and sharing their opinions of design.
