PETER SAVILLE
Peter Saville is an English art director and graphic designer. He came to fame for the many record sleeves he designed for Factory Records, of which he was a director. He best known for his album covers for David Bowie.
He has a lot of experience in vinyl album design. He cites record cover artwork as an early design influence, and possibly the interest that drew him to graphic design, with other covers including ones for John Foxx and Tuxedomoon.
