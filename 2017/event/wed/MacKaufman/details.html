Welcome to SHAKE AND CREATE!

This is an event brought to you buy graphic design sponsors all over the East Coast in attempt to pull together artists such
as design directors, senior designers and junior designers in order to create the “perfect team”. While offering a chance for
different artists’ styles to come together, the goal is for everyone to build a relationship in hopes to collaborate together in the
near future.

Who created this?

Mac Kaufman, a student at the Maryland Institute College of Art, found
that as a young person in the Graphic Design department she wasn’t
given the chance to collaborate with other artists in her department.
Assignment after assignment all we’re given is the option to work alone
while our professors seemed to forget the concept of learning from
others. We are as people are always growing and especially as impres
sionable individuals, it is now that we can are given the opportunity to
learn and benefit from working together.
