ABOUT
The User’s Festival is a interactive design festival. It focus on the expierence of the users and discuss about the influence of simplicity and complexity in interactive design. We invited three speakers who are veteran in interactive design. They will talk about their experience and ideas about simplicity and complexity. We would like to introduce interactive design to everyone and let more people be aware of the existence of UX. We want to privide an opportunity to people who don’t familiar with design and interaction. This festival is also a place for designers
to communicate.
