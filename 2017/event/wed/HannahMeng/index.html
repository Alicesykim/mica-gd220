How Should We Live?
How Should We Live? Propositions for the Modern Interior Conference will be on view September 23, 2017 at The New Museum. The exhibition will examine a range of environments - interiors, exhibition displays, and furnitue designs - with the aim of exploring the complex collaborative partnerships, materials, and processes that have shaped the modernist interior.

Structure is a forthcoming exhibition for Milan Design Week showcasing contemporary Norwegian craft and design.
