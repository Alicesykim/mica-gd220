ABOUT

The first ever 24hr Talks is a citywide celebration of design and creativity. This year, our theme is Designers vs. World, through programming and events, you will explore the various ways design is shaping the way we live and work. From the workplace to the built environment, thoughtful design can impact and even change the way we experience the world around us.
