Fashion magazine cover festival is an event - free for everyone - dedicated to graphic design. It
aims to be a meeting place for the discipline’s actors and a platform for discovering innovations
and practices through a program for professionals, students, young graphic designers, and gen-
eral public split across different series of events. We are looking for graphic designers who have
a passionate and creative idea of designing our magazine cover. You are able to apply for 3 dif
ferent magazine cover design but with different designs. Especially the festival is the one, not
only you can upload your design covers and also get the opportunity to be our film's designer
as well.

For 30 days between May 1st and 30th, you are able to apply for this festival. Anna Wintour,
Laura Brown, and Roberta Myers will have talks about their style of the magazine and what
kind of styles they want for your design. We are excited and extremely looking for your work.
