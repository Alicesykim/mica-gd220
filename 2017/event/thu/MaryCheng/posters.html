CHINESE FILM POSTER FESTIVAL

Chinese Film Poster Festival is an international festival of film poster and film market. Its interest
lies in the artistic and economic implications of new technologies. The event gathers people of
all backgrounds from all around the world. They are designers, directors, makers, screenwriter,
artists, architects, developers or musicians. They come to Chinese Film Poster Festival to tell you
their personal anecdotes, to share professional experiences and innovative ideas, to present an
artwork, a project or a product. You return home full of inspiration with plenty of contacts and
new passions.
