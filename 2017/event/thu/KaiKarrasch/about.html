Green Design

May 8, 2017   10am - 7pm.


UCSF. 530 Parnassus Ave
San Francisco, CA 94143


As designers we all have a voice and a chance to make an impact
on many enviromental and social issues. Therefore the main diss-
cussion of this conference is around the idea, how can we use de-
sign to educate people on major enviromental issues?

With this idea in mind everyone will be able to listen to three in-
spirational speakers talk about their experience as well as proj-
ects. We are fortunate enough to welcome Ellen McMahon, Annie
Leonard, and Eric Rodenbeck. These speakers have all used their
voices in different ways to educate people on enviromental issues

Come join us at UCSF to learn more about how to make a change.
