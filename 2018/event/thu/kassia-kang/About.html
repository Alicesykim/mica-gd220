Think Colorful Festival celebrates and promotes in the New York as the design capital of the United States. The festival will hold in two more cities, Los Angeles and Chicago from the 13-15 June 2018.
A unique collaboration between the contemporary installation to web design. Over the past nine years, Color in Design Festival has spread idea of effective color usage throughout the art. The festival would fill with an unique range of large and small, intricate and majestic designs.
Every June, partners from all across the world, from emerging studios to established companies and educational institutions, come together to show off their latest works and ideas.
