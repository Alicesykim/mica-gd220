m design festival

m design festival is a discussion of minimalism. As a design style and also a life style, Minimalism exists everywhere. It is one of the design trends that just never gets old. The simplicity of it makes minimalism easy to incorporate into a number of other styles and trends. It is classic and classy.

We invite three japanese deisgners from different design areas to talk about their works, their principles and their ways to see this world. We think these background differences are valuable and can bring us a more versatile consideration about minimalism.
