About:

A collection of some of the very best brand identity logo design work for art galleries and exhibitions, reviewed and published on LogoE. This post features encompasses simple logo and stationery projects, and extends to broader brand identity programmes that include interior graphics and way finding, catalogues, flyers and poster. These effectively play with colour, form and type, and work in high quality material detail and finishes.
