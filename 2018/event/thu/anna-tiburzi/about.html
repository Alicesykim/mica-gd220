Designing album art can be difficult and hard to approach at times, as you are often very limited with the information you are able to work with. How does one learn to properly communiate ideas and possible concepts with the musical artist? How is one supposed to “advertise” an album? How are we supposed to describe or represent an album with cover art?

Questions and concerns like these, among plenty of others, will be adressed at this event! There will be a Q&A and workshops with the guest speakers.

Refreshments will be provided throughout the day and there will be representatives of various design companies attending the event.
