Jesse Kanda
Jesse Kanda is a self taught artist who grew up in Japan, and now
works and lives in London. He had provided designs, visuals,
and animations for various music artists on both album covers and
music videos. He is known for creating grotesque, surreal, and
uncomfortable visuals based off his fascinaion with the repulsive,
the other-worldly, death, and how things decay.
Vlad Septov
Vlad Septov is a graphic designer known for creating many album
cover designs for various hip-hop artists. His most iconic work was
the album covers he Ddid for Kendrick Lamar on “To Pimp A Butterfly”
and “DAMN.” He has also worked for ScHoolboy Q, J. Cole,
Jay Rock, and Mac Dre.
Amy Burrows
Amy Burrows has experience with album design, logo design,
and various print design. She is the experimental art director
in charge of all of CHVRCHES album designs throughout their career.
