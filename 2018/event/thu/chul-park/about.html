discuss about why people prefer minimalist design.
We will have three lectures from each speaker. If you
have any questions or want to learn about graphic
design and minimalist design, come and learn about
minimalist design and why it matters. This event is
taking place at mica falvey hall during 11am to 4pm
on october, 15th, 2018.
