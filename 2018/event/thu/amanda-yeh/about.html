About
VRUX is a design conference all about designing user experience in virtual reality. Experts in the field cover topics on accessibility, interface design, product testing and design processes in virtual reality. We think that virtual reality is more than a 2018 buzzword, but a rising medium for work and play, where sharp design is crucial
