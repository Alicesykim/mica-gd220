    ABOUT

    Nowadays, many designers do not have the time or energy to travel. This event is to provide an opportunity for designers to have a chance to leave their studio and learn about an interesting place by themselves. We hope that designers can incorporate the inspiration gained from travel into their own designs. So we choose Jerusalem as the target place, where contains both history and unique culture.
