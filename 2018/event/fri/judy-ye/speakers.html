STEFAN SAGMEISTER

Stefan Sagmeister is a New York-based graphic designer, storyteller, and typographer. Sagmeister co-founded a design firm called Sagmeister & Walsh Inc. with Jessica Walsh in New York City. He has designed album covers for Lou Reed, OK Go, The Rolling Stones, David Byrne, Jay Z, Aerosmith and Pat Metheny.

https://sagmeisterwalsh.com/

SHANE GRIFFIN

Shane Griffin is an Irish born Director & Artist. His work spans a broad range of disciplines, from animation and live action, to sculpture and CGI. Shane has created work for some of the worlds leading brands & agencies. He's directed films for Apple, Google, Microsoft, Ford, and Adidas. Shane is currently executive creative director at Los York Studio & director at Los York Films.

http://www.shanegriffin.nyc/

WANG ZHIHONG

Wang Zhi-Hong, a leading graphic designer based in Taiwan and a member of Alliance Graphique Internationale (AGI) since 2015. Born in 1975 in Taipei. Graduated from Fu-Hsin Trade and Art School in 1995. Started his studio in 2000 and offering design solutions in fields of publication, art, architecture, film and music.

http://wangzhihong.com/
