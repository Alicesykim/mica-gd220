Most designers have taken a course or are familiar with color theory.

 

Choosing the right colors is very important because color can affect the entire outcome of your design. It’s highly recommended that before committing to specific colors in your design, you do the research to make sure those colors will convey the message you want to send. Research has been done to show how different colors influence people’s emotions and actions. If you’re open to the colors used for your design, consider choosing colors based on how they’re perceived.

 

Our event will help people know more about the importance of colors in design.
