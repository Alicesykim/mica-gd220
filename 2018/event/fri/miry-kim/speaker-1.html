LEANNE FREAMAR

Leanne Fremar is senior vice president and executive creative director. In this role, Leanne leads the creative development of all Starbucks global brand initiatives and marketing campaigns including digital creative and packaging, as well as oversight of the branding, creative and marketing of Starbucks Roasteries and the Starbucks Reserve brand. Leanne leads a team of more than 100 Starbucks partners at Starbucks in-house Creative Studio in Seattle, and its external creative agencies.
