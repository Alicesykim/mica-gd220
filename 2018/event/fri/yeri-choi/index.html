Design Essentials

– An Event Addressing the Foundational Role of the Designer Inside the Interdisciplinary Field of Design –
About
DESIGN ESSENTIALS MICA Falvey Hall / 1301 W Mt Royal Ave, Baltimore, MD 21217 July 24, 2017 / 12:00 – 19:00 CONTACT (410) 669-9200 / Help@MICA.edu
A One Day Design Event that addresses the role of the designer inside the contemporary interdisciplinary field of design. There will be several programs and initiatives, counting with three guest speakers that are influential in today’s design world: Nick Schaden, Libby Bawcombe, and Dokho Shin. Through this event, we intent to inspire young artists and designers to stay attentive to the Design Essentials and explore a variety of creative, unique, and practical approaches.
