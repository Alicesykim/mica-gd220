Complication but Simple...

“Com Sim” is an artist’s talk event, which focuses on how artists
simplify complicated situations in reality. There is no definite set of,
emotion such as anger, sadness, and happiness. However, our feeling
are always belong from combination of multiple emotions. The artists
will share thier story about how they understand thier complicated
emotional status and how they visually simplify thier feelings.
