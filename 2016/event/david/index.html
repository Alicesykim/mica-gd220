Die Zukunft

MIC/A's Brown Center > 1301 W Mt Royal Ave, Baltimore, MD 21217 // November 23, 2016 // 1:00 > 4:00

Join us on November 23rd for an exciting look into the world of German design. Three of Germany's finest young designers, Malo-Malo, Luca di Battista and Isabell Neher will host an exhibition and round-table discussion to showcase their talents, expertise and international perspective on the changing landscape of design.
